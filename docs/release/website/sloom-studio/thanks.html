Genuinely — thank you for paying for Signal Loom. A solo dev felt that. Your desktop build is right here — grab whichever fits your machine. (On Android? It's coming to the Samsung Galaxy Store & Google Play — the proper, trusted way to get it.)

Windows 10 / 11 Installer (.exe) NSIS installer, 64-bit. ~148 MB. Download for Windows
Any Linux AppImage Portable — chmod +x and run. ~171 MB. Download AppImage
Debian / Ubuntu Package (.deb) Installs via apt / dpkg. ~127 MB. Download .deb
On a Mac? There's no native Mac build yet (it needs a signed build on Apple hardware — coming later). For now you can run Signal Loom in your browser or from source — see the docs. Treat that route as experimental and untested on macOS.

First launch, Windows SmartScreen may warn that the publisher is unverified — that's normal for an independent app without a paid signing certificate; choose More info → Run anyway. On Linux, make the AppImage executable (chmod +x). Verify your download against the SHA-256 checksums. Same bring-your-own-key model — Signal Loom never sees your keys or your work. Anything misbehaves? Email support@sloom.studio and it gets fixed fast.
