Help bring the Signal Loom Android build to Google Play

Signal Loom is finishing Google Play's closed test. Before the Android & Samsung DeX build can go live, Google requires 12 testers to have it installed for 14 days. If you have an Android device, you can help — and you get early access to the full studio.

This is the official Google Play test, not a sideloaded APK. You install from the Play Store like any app, through your own Google account.

What's involved

  • You need a real Android device and a genuine Google account (no emulators).
  • Once you're approved, you'll get a private opt-in link to join the test and install from Google Play.
  • Just leave it installed for the 14 days — you don't have to use it daily (though feedback is very welcome).
  • Samsung DeX and S Pen users especially wanted — the build has native support for both.

Why a form instead of posting your email publicly

So you never have to paste your email into a public thread. This form sends your application straight to the developer's inbox over an encrypted connection — it isn't stored in any database, shown to other testers, or shared with anyone. Other testers never see who else applied.

Apply

The Gmail / Google account you use on your Android device — this is the address Google adds to the test. Nothing else works for Play testing.
