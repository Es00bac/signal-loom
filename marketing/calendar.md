# 2-week launch cadence

I produce each drop; you approve + send (minutes, not hours). Times are guides — consistency beats
perfection. I can set reminders (ScheduleWakeup) to resurface each item on the day.

## Week 0 — prep (no public posts yet)
- **Accounts:** Reddit, X, Discord created; X bio + pinned set; (if selling direct) itch.io or Gumroad page up.
- **Discord:** seed it (own showcase posts, pinned intro, first dev-log), invite your 12 testers.
- **Reddit warm-up:** ~20 min/day genuine comments in r/StableDiffusion + r/SideProject (NO app
  mentions) — builds the history that makes the launch post survive.
- **Assets:** demo GIF (idea → comic page), 4–5 screenshots, feature graphic.
- **Play:** ASO listing live (done); internal testing track up so you can install.
- **Buffer/Typefully:** connect your X; load the build-in-public queue.

## Launch week
- **Mon** — final check: demo GIF ready, Discord not empty, links work. Soft "launching this week" tease on X.
- **Tue 9am local** — 🚀 **r/SideProject** post + **X launch thread** (pin). Be live for 2 hours; paste me every comment.
- **Wed 8–9am ET** — **Show HN** on Hacker News. Build-in-public tweet. Reply to yesterday's threads.
- **Thu** — **r/StableDiffusion** (a result you made + workflow, not a pitch). Tweet #2.
- **Fri** — schedule **Product Hunt** for next Tue; post an **itch.io devlog**; tweet #3 (a transparency/learnings post).

## Week 2
- **Tue 12:01am PT** — **Product Hunt** launch. Rally the Discord + testers to engage early. Be present all day.
- **Wed/Thu** — **r/androidapps** + a comic sub (r/comicmaking/r/webcomics) with a page made in Paper.
- **Fri** — **weekly transparency tweet**: real numbers (downloads, sales, what worked). These overperform.
- **Daily** — 1 build-in-public post; reply to everything; keep the Discord alive.

## Ongoing rhythm (after launch)
- 1 build-in-public post/day, 1 transparency post/week, ship + post a visible improvement weekly.
- Every new result/feature → showcase in Discord → clip to X → occasional relevant subreddit.
- The compounding loop: visible progress → followers → installs/ratings → Play's own discovery wakes up.
