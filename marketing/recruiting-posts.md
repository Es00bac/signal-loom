# Tester recruiting — ready-to-paste (goal: 12+ Gmails for the Play closed test)

**Order of operations (Play):** collect tester **Gmails** now → create the app in Play Console →
set up a **Closed testing** track → add the emails (or a Google Group) → roll out → the **14-day**
clock starts. So the job right now is just **collecting emails**; no opt-in link exists yet.

**Recruit ~15–18, not 12** — free testers flake (opt out early, use emulators). Real Android devices
only count. Reciprocate (test theirs back) — it's the norm and it works.
